This is the Ciudadano Inteligente NGO website. The challenge was to create a site that requires minimal maintenance effort (the team is always a bit overwhelmed with projects), to explain what the NGO is doing since what we do is a bit complex for unfamiliar people, and to highlight our projects as the most important. It's a Github page, where every project and team member are pages in markdown, so they can be created and edited by any member of the team.
