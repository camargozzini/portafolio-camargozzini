Rediseño del sitio de la ONG Ciudadanía Inteligente, cuya misión es fortalecer la democracia y reducir la desigualdad en América Latina a través del uso innovativo de las tecnologías de información.

This is the Ciudadano Inteligente NGO website. The challenge was to create a site that requires minimal maintenance effort (the team is always a bit overwhelmed with projects), to explain who we are and how we do what the NGO does is doing since what we do is a bit complex for unfamiliar people, and to highlight our projects as the most important. It's a Github page, where every project and team member are pages in markdown, so they can be created and edited by any member of the team.
